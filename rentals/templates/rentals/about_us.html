About Us

Games are great for having fun, relaxing, bonding and exercising one’s mind. They can however be a strain on your wallet.

Game On is a platform where individual gamers and groups can:

  • Rent video games and party games to play. (Why spend so much money on a game you’ll finish in one week or play at a games night for just a few hours?)
  • Rent out their idle games to others to earn a passive income. (Instead of your completed or idle games sitting on your shelf, why not have them generate income for you and bring joy to others?)
  • Buy Games and Gaming related items
